# Key Learnings from AI Job Red Flag Detection using claude:

- Learned how to use AI to systematically analyze job descriptions and identify potential red flags before applying.
- Discovered that job titles do not always match actual expectations; some "junior" roles may demand senior-level responsibilities and experience.
- Understood the importance of evaluating workload indicators such as on-call support, excessive technology requirements, and multi-role expectations.
- Learned to assess company stability by reviewing factors such as team size, funding status, growth stage, and transparency around compensation.
- Realized that remote-work claims should be carefully reviewed for relocation clauses or other hidden conditions.
- Identified common culture-related warning signs in job descriptions, including phrases that may indicate high-pressure environments or unclear work-life boundaries.
- Learned to balance red flags with positive signals such as learning opportunities, technology exposure, and broad responsibilities.
- Understood the value of asking strategic interview questions to uncover information about compensation, career growth, workload, remote policies, and company stability.
- Gained experience using AI as a decision-support tool to perform risk assessment and make more informed career decisions.
- Developed a framework for evaluating job opportunities beyond salary and title by considering culture, growth potential, stability, and role clarity.
