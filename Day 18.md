# Key Learnings :

1. Converting Unstructured Notes into Actionable Insights
Learned how to transform lengthy meeting transcripts into a structured dashboard containing summaries, action items, risks, conflicts, open questions, and speaker-wise decisions.
2. Information Extraction and Categorization
Implemented prompt engineering techniques to automatically identify:
Key takeaways
Action items with owners and deadlines
Risks and blockers
Open questions
Decision points
Speaker contributions
3. Prioritization of Tasks
Learned how to distinguish between informational statements and actionable tasks, enabling the system to generate a focused action plan from raw discussions.
4. Stakeholder Analysis
Built logic to track individual speaker contributions, responsibilities, concerns, and decisions, improving accountability and meeting transparency.
5. Risk and Dependency Detection
Extracted potential blockers, unresolved issues, and project dependencies from conversations, helping teams identify challenges early.
6. Dashboard-Oriented AI Output Design
Learned how to structure AI-generated outputs into business-friendly sections that are easy to scan and consume rather than providing long text summaries.
7. Prompt Engineering for Structured Outputs
Improved skills in designing prompts that consistently generate JSON/structured data, which can then be rendered into interactive dashboards.
8. Business Meeting Intelligence
Discovered how AI can assist managers and teams by automatically generating:
Executive summaries
Follow-up actions
Decision logs
Ownership tracking
Meeting accountability reports
9. Data Visualization Thinking
Learned how to organize extracted insights into visual cards, tables, status indicators, and categorized sections to improve readability and decision-making.
10. Real-World AI Automation Workflow
Gained practical experience in building an AI-powered productivity tool that automates meeting analysis, reducing manual note-taking and follow-up effort.
