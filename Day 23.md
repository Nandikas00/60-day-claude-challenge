# Today, I built my Customer & MVP Blueprint using claude :

## Key Learnings :

* Gained a deeper understanding of the primary target audience by defining clear customer personas, their pain points, motivations, and willingness to pay.
* Learned that interview preparation, skill gap identification, and personalized career guidance are the most critical problems faced by students and fresh graduates.
* Understood the importance of validating assumptions through customer interviews, pricing surveys, and early user feedback before investing in full-scale product development.
* Learned how to prioritize MVP features using the MoSCoW framework, focusing only on high-impact features such as AI Mock Interviews, Skill Gap Assessment, and Personalized Learning Roadmaps.
* Identified major business risks, including uncertain willingness to pay, single-founder dependency, and competition, along with practical mitigation strategies.
* Understood the value of defining a clear customer journey to improve user acquisition, engagement, retention, and referrals.
* Learned that achieving product-market fit requires real user validation, paying customers, and continuous iteration rather than relying solely on assumptions.
* Recognized the importance of building an MVP quickly, collecting user feedback, and improving the product based on measurable outcomes before seeking funding.
